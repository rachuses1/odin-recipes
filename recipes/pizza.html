Pimply Pizza

This is the perfect pizza to have when you are feeling like you want to be filthy

Ingredients

    Pizza dough Pepperoni Mozzarella Cheese Tomato Sauce Mushrooms

Steps

    Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees celsius Roll the dough to whatever shape you want it to be Spread that tomato sauce on thic Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top like it is Christmas and it is snowing Add some of that pepperoni and mushroom goodness on top Put the pizza into the oven and wait 20 minutes Enjoy the delicious pimply pizza
