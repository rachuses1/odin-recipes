Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Your favourite meat

Steps

  1. Put some water in a pot and put a generous amount of salt in
  2. Boil the water
  3. When water is boiling, pour pasta into the water
  4. when pasta is cooked, strain the pasta and pour into a mixing bowl
  5. Toss in the rest of the ingredients and mix
  6. Serve in a fancy plate
